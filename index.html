Providing computer literacy classes and 4IR courses.

Tech Vibes is dedicated to providing computer literacy classes and 4IR courses, including coding, machine learning, 3D printing, gaming, and drones, to grade 7 and grade 10 learners in poor and disadvantaged schools.

To empower learners from disadvantaged schools.

Tech Vibes envisions a future where every learner from disadvantaged schools has access to computer literacy classes and 4IR courses, empowering them with the skills needed for employability and community innovation.
